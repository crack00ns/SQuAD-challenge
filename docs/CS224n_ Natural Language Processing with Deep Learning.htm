Milestone

The milestone will be graded credit/no-credit based on whether you turned it in on time. To complete the milestone, you must train a model using GPUs (most likely on Azure) and submit it to the CodaLab dev set leaderboard. Your submitted model can be the provided baseline, or it can be a model you've developed. Your F1 and EM score will not be taken into account when awarding your credit for the completed milestone. Note that these are the minimum requirements for your milestone. Ideally, you will have made more progress than this by the due date of the milestone.
